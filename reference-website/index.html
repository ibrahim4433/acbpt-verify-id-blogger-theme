Who we are

The American Canadian Board for Professional Training

The American Canadian Board for Professional Training – A private for-profit training institution – Registered with the US Department of Commerce – Delaware.

The board consists of various training entities registered in the US and Canada – owned by a multinational board of directors.

We work towards supporting training institutions and those working in the human performance development field such as trainers, coaches, and consultants.
